About Us

Founded in 2010, Monochrome Photography specializes in capturing the world in black and white. Our work is driven by a passion for light, shadow, and the timeless beauty of monochromatic imagery.

We believe that removing color allows the viewer to focus on the essential elements of a photograph - composition, texture, emotion, and the interplay of light and shadow. This minimalist approach reveals the true essence of our subjects.

Our team of photographers brings together diverse backgrounds in fine art, documentary, and commercial photography, united by a shared vision of creating powerful, evocative imagery.

Based in New York but working worldwide, we approach every project with artistic integrity and technical excellence.
